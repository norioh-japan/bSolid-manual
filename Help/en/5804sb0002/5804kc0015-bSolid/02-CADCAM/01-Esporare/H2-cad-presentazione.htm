Information on environment

The Cad/Camenvironment is dedicated to creating documentsElectronic worksheet on which the geometric and technological parameters are inserted.: graphic projects to which instructions for machining wood are attributed (see Managing the documents).

Any type of geometric elementAn abstract element associated with specific properties: the point, line, plane and space are all geometric elements. can be drawn in this environment:

  •    elementary figures and outlines (i.e. lines, arcs, circles, texts and freehand forms with homogeneous curves);
  •    complex three-dimensional figures (cylinders, cubes, toroids, surfacesFigures of a three-dimensional area, that represent a thin open or closed structure. of various shapes, etc.).

The construction of three-dimensional figures requires the creation of single two-dimensional geometric elements and/or flat figures.

Using the tools of the CAD options barBar listing the optional tools useful for drawing (e.g. the commands to rotate or resize the drawing, etc.), you can perform other operations:

• move, rotate, scale and create mirror copies;
• cut and extend the geometric elements;
• add chamferingChamfering produces a corner. The chamfer can be linear or rounded. and connectorsIn the technical drawing, a straight line or arc that eliminates the sharp corner between two consecutive non-tangent elements. Synonym of "chamfer"., etc.
