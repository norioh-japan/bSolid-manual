Lead-out

Brings together the data concerning the movement of the gluing(1) Set of operations for attaching the edgebanding strip to the piece. unit at the end of the machining operation, and allows you to programme the following operations:

  •    distancing of the glue roller and presser roller from the piece;
  •    end of gluing operation on closed profile;
  •    ascent of the gluing unitMachine part that attaches the edgebanding strip to the piece..

These data can be used to establish the detachment point, pressingOperation to compress an object, material, etc. with a special tool (e.g. presser, presser roller, etc.). point, and ascent point:

  •    Detachment point: the point on the profile where the presser roller is positioned at the end of the pressing operation, before moving away from the profile.
  •    Pressing point: the point on the profile where the presser roller is positioned at the end of the machining operation, to apply pressure on the final part of the edgebandingSet of operations for covering the edge of the piece (gluing, cleaning, rounding off, etc.). strip applied (or on the joint, in the case of closed ring edgebanding).
  •    Ascent point: the point in space where the gluing unit is positioned when, after the machining operation, it moves upwards.

Calibration

CAL

Used for the preparation of the edgebanding strip to be applied to the joint.

If the box is tickedClick on a check box to tick it and thereby enable that function., predefined values are automatically entered in data fields "Joint correction" and data fields "Extra edgebanding strip on closure" so as to generate a test programme that you can launch on the machine.

Joint correction

ETD

Distance to be edgebanded on the closed profile, between the joint (end of the profile) and the edge trimming point.

Alter this fieldA defined area containing data or commands (data field or command field). to adjust an incorrect joint

Extra edgebanding strip on closure

EXB

Increase in the amount of edgebanding strip dispensed from the magazine. This minimises the wastePart left over after a machining operation (residue). In nesting projects this definition also applies to waste. resulting from the second cut, made to adjust the joint of the two ends of the strip applied to the closed profile.

Pre-edge trimming speed

ETS

Interpolation speed of the gluing unit axes during the positioning of the unit in the edge trimming point.

Glue roller detachment

Brings together the data for moving the gluing unit in order to move the glue roller away from the piece.

These data can be used to establish the detachment start and end points.

B. Detachment start point: the point on the profile where the glue roller is positioned to begin its movement away from the piece.
C. Detachment end point: the point in space where the glue roller is positioned after moving away from the piece.

Presser roller detachment and distancing
NOTES:

Section relating to the machining of open profiles only (open ring edgebanding).

Brings together the data for moving the gluing unit in order to terminate the machining operation and distance the unit from the edgebanded piece.

These data can be used to establish the detachment point, pressing point, and ascent point:

  •    Detachment point: the point on the profile where the presser roller is positioned at the end of the pressing operation, before moving away from the profile.
  •    Pressing point: the point on the profile where the presser roller is positioned at the end of the machining operation, to apply pressure on the final part of the edgebanding strip applied (or on the joint, in the case of closed ring edgebanding).
  •    Ascent point: the point in space where the gluing unit is positioned when, after the machining operation, it moves upwards.
