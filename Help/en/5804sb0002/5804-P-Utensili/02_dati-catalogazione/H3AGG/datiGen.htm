Type of aggregate

Type of aggregate. Choose the type in the drop-down menuList of commands (to be selected with the mouse) that develop a sub-menu, i.e. a list of options that allow certain functions/operations to be carried out..

Rotation

RotDir

Rotation direction of the aggregate spindle. Choose one of the following options from the drop-down menu:

Clockwise

Clockwise rotation.

Ccwise

Anti-clockwise rotation.

Auto

Automatic rotation on the basis of the tool rotation direction.

Offset
398. Example:
A. Electrospindle.

C. Point of reference or centre of rotation of the aggregate, which usually coincides with the centre of rotation of the electrospindle.

X offset

OffsetX

Distance in X from the electrospindle centre of rotation for establishing the reference point C of the aggregate (figure 1).

Only enter the distance if the aggregate centre of rotation is different from that of the electrospindle. Otherwise, leave the valuePredefined / pre-set. Standard information useful for the control, if the requested information is not entered. at 0.

Y offset

OffsetY

Distance in Y from the electrospindle centre of rotation for establishing the reference point C of the aggregate (figure 1).

Only enter the distance if the aggregate centre of rotation is different from that of the electrospindle. Otherwise, leave the value at 0.

Z offset

OffsetZ

Distance in Z from the electrospindle centre of rotation for establishing the reference point C of the aggregate (figure 1).

Only enter the distance if the aggregate centre of rotation is different from that of the electrospindle. Otherwise, leave the value at 0.

Active X offset

ActiveOffsetX

Coordinate in the direction of the X axis. Any value other than zero indicates that the aggregate has certain releasing elements so it can alter its position during the machining operation.

Active Y offset

ActiveOffsetY

Coordinate in the direction of the Y axis. Any value other than zero indicates that the aggregate has certain releasing elements so it can alter its position during the machining operation.

Active Z offset

ActiveOffsetZ

Coordinate in the direction of the Z axis. Any value other than zero indicates that the aggregate has certain releasing elements so it can alter its position during the machining operation.
