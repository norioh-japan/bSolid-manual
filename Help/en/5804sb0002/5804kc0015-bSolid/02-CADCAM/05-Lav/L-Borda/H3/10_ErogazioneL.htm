Liquid delivery

Description of the machining operation

Allows to dispense a non-adhesive liquid with the appropriate edgebanding deviceDevice for carrying out edgebanding operations on the piece..

326. Representation of the machining operation

This machining operation allows to spread a special liquid on the side of the piece before applying the glue, to avoid leaving excessive sticky deposits on the edges of the piece to edgeband.
