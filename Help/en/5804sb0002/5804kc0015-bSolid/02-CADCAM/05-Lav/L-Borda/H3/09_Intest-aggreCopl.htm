Edge trimming/Rounding with GROTEFELD copier aggregate

Description of the machining operation

Used to cut the edgebandingSet of operations for covering the edge of the piece (gluing, cleaning, rounding off, etc.). strip at the ends of the edgebanded side, and round off the corners of the edgebanded piece, using the aggregate(tool) Device fitted with tools. with copier (GROTEFELD model).

Reversal

Enables (box tickedClick on a check box to tick it and thereby enable that function.) or disables a reversal of the machining direction.

To perform the machining operation, the aggregate does not begin from the initial point of the profile, but from the end point. Only used if the profile has been generated in the anti-clockwise direction.

Reverse the cutting direction

ICR

Forces (box ticked) the aggregate blade to make the cut by inverting its direction: from the bottom upwards or from the top downwards.

Collision

CCC

Activates (box ticked) or deactivates the check on the moving parts and parts being machined, to avoid any possible interference or collision.

Working speed

WSP

Advance speed of the aggregate during the machining operation.

Blade radius correction

BRC

Blade radius correction when moving into and out of the edgebanding strip to be cut.

This valuePredefined / pre-set. Standard information useful for the control, if the requested information is not entered. allows you to make a sham variation of the blade dimensions, to compensate any data inserted and to correct the position of the piece in relation to the position of the blade.

Rounding thickness threshold

ETHR

Minimum edgebanding strip thickness in order to perform corner trimmingRounding off the corners of the edgebanded piece..

If the thickness of the edgebanding strip is less than this value, the aggregate will perform the edge trimming(1) Piece edge trimming: operation for making cuts on the side faces (heads) in order to square the piece. (2) Border edge trimming: cuts at the ends of the edgebanded side. only (without rounding off the corner).

Rounding

EXR

Enables (box ticked) or disables the rounding of the sharp corner, using the relevant copiers in the aggregate.

Descent start

VDE

Used to establish the initial point of the cut, where the blade increases its advance speed.

Descent end

QPV

Used to establish the final point of blade descent, at the end of the cut.
