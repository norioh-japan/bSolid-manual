Tab Data

Offset
X offset

OffsetX

Distance in X from the electrospindle rotation centre, for establishing the deflector rotation centre.

Only enter the distance if the deflector centre of rotation is different from that of the electrospindle. Otherwise, leave the valuePredefined / pre-set. Standard information useful for the control, if the requested information is not entered. at 0.

Y offset

OffsetY

Distance in Y from the electrospindle rotation centre, for establishing the deflector rotation centre.

Only enter the distance if the deflector centre of rotation is different from that of the electrospindle. Otherwise, leave the value at 0.

Z offset

OffsetZ

Distance in Z from the electrospindle rotation centre, for establishing the deflector rotation centre.

Only enter the distance if the deflector centre of rotation is different from that of the electrospindle. Otherwise, leave the value at 0.

Parameters
Direction

Direction

Deflector rotation direction.

RIGHT

Right-hand deflector.

Left

Left-hand deflector.

Distance

Distance

Deflector radius.

AZ

AZ

Tilt angle of the deflector curtain guard.

AR

AR

Rotation angle of the deflector curtain guard (ref. A, figure ).

For example, let's suppose that the curtain guard rotation angle is 18.3. In this case, we have to calculate 90+18.3=108.3 for the right-hand deflector and 270-18.3=251.3 for the left-hand deflector.

Integrated on the tool

IsEmbedded

If the box is tickedClick on a check box to tick it and thereby enable that function., this indicates that the deflector is incorporated in the milling tool.

In this case, enable the "Enable integrated deflector" box in the data of the specific tool fitted with a deflector, and choose the deflector in the "Integrated Deflector" data field.
