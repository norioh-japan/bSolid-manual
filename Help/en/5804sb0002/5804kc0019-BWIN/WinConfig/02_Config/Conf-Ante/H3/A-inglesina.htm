Muntin

Section of muntin elements

MUNTIN(doors/windows) Grid made up of horizontal and/or vertical strips to be applied to the glass to create a purely decorative effect._SECTION

Width A of the wood strips for muntins.

489. Width A

 

Total thickness of muntin pieces

MUNTIN_ELEMENT_TOTAL_THICKNESS

Thickness of the piece of wood to obtain two overlapping strips for the muntins.

Overlap of muntin elements

MUNTIN_OVERLAY

Part of the muntin strip that overlaps the glazing bead when the two elements are joined together.

Milling angle

MUNTIN_ROUT_ANGLE

Angle of the machining operation (on the glazing bead) in order to generate the insertion seat with the muntin strip.

Milling length correction

MUNTIN_ROUT_DELTA_L

Correction on the width A (figure 1) of the muntin strip, to increase or decrease the insertion seat created on the glazing bead strip.

Y milling position

MUNTIN_ROUT_Y

Height of the insertion seat created on the glazing bead strip.

Y low milling position

MUNTIN_ROUT_Y_LOW

Height of the insertion seat created on the lower side of the glazing bead.

Correction of high milling depth

MUNTIN_ROUT_DELTA_DP_UP

Correction of the machining depth programmed to machine the sash/door that opens into the environment.

Correction of low milling depth

MUNTIN_ROUT_DELTA_DP_LOW

Correction of the machining depth programmed to machine the sash/door that opens toward the exterior of the environment.

Method for joining the muntin with the glazing bead

MUNTIN_GLAZINGBEAD_JUNCTION

Used to choose the type of joint: joint with dowels, or joint with seats; to join glazing bead to the muntins.

Muntin application method

MUNTIN_APPLICATIONMODE

Used to choose where to apply the strips for muntins.

Inside only

The strips are glued onto the glass surfaceFigures of a three-dimensional area, that represent a thin open or closed structure., facing in towards the room.

Outside only

The strips are glued onto the glass surface, facing outwards.

Inside and outside

The strips are glued on both sides of the glass surface.
