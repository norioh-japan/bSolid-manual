Sequences table

Shows a list of the sequences created. Each line displays a particular sequence.

Click(1) Indicates the action of pressing (and immediately releasing) the mouse button on an object (icon, button, etc.) located under the pointer on the screen. (2) (verb) Pressing and immediately releasing the left mouse button to enable the function of a selected command. on a row of the table to visualise the card with the data of the sequence.

Preview box

Shows a list of the machining operations belonging to the machining sequence selected in the table.
