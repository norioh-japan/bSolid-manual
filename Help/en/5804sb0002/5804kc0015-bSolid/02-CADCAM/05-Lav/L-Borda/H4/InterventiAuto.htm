691. Representation of the data

Distance from curve

DASI

Distance from the concave profile for establishing the point in which the speed defined in "Speed variation" should be applied.

Examples:

  •    If a value of 0 is entered in this field, the speed variation begins when the glue roller is on the initial point of the concave profile and ends when it is on the final point.
  •    If a value of 10 is entered in this field, the speed variation begins when the glue roller is 10 millimetres after the initial point and ends when it is 10 millimetres after the final point.
