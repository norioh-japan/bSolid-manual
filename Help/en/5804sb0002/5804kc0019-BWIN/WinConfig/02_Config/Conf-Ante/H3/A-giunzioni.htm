Joints

Edge trimming excess material
Cutting excess material for counterprofile joint

OVERMATERIAL_CUT_COUNTERPROFILE

Excess material(nesting) Type of material to be associated with the articles and sheets of the nesting project. to be removed from the side faces of the piece, to create the counterprofile.

Cutting excess material for Minizinken joint

OVERMATERIAL_CUT_MINIZINKEN

Excess material to be removed from the side faces of the piece for joints made with the minizinken millMill for 45° joints, producing a toothed shape (with notches)..

Cutting excess material for flat joint

OVERMATERIAL_CUT_FLAT

Excess material to be removed from the side faces of the piece, for the flat joints.

Cut excess material for bearing tenon joint

OVERMATERIAL_CUT_OPENTENON

Excess material to be removed from the side faces of the piece, for the joints with tenon and mortice.

Cut excess material for receiving tenon joint

OVERMATERIAL_CUT_OPENTENON_RECEIVING

Excess material to be removed from the side faces of the piece, for the joints with tenon and mortice.

Excess material cut for pocketing joint

OVERMATERIAL_CUT_POCKETING

Excess material to be removed from the side faces of the door elements for joints with pocketing of the receiving part.

Excess material cut for vertical counterprofile joint

OVERMATERIAL_CUT_VERTICAL_COUNTERPROFILE

Excess material to be removed from the side faces of the door elements for joints with the counterprofile.

Default
Default for upper joints

JUNCTIONS_UPPER_NONTANGENT

Used to choose the type of joint for uniting the upper crosspiece(doors/windows) Also: crosspiece. Horizontal part of the window/door. with the uprights.

Default for lower joints

JUNCTIONS_LOWER_NONTANGENT

Used to choose the type of joint for uniting the lower crosspiece with the uprights.

Default for joints between heads

JUNCTIONS_TANGENT

Used to choose the type of joint for uniting two window element heads (e.g. two sections, an arc and an upright, etc.).

Default for central joints

JUNCTIONS_CENTRAL

Used to choose the type of joint for uniting the central crosspiece with the uprights.

Default for muntin joints

JUNCTIONS_MUNTIN(doors/windows) Grid made up of horizontal and/or vertical strips to be applied to the glass to create a purely decorative effect.

Used to choose the type of joint for uniting two window element heads (e.g. two sections, an arc and an upright, etc.).

Effect
Effect of upper joints

JUNCTIONS_UPPER_INCIDENCE

Used to choose the type of joint between the upright and the upper crosspiece. Choose joint A or joint B (figure 1).

484. Effects
A. joining of crosspieces against uprights;
B. joining of uprights against crosspieces.
Effect of lower joints

JUNCTIONS_LOWER_INCIDENCE

Used to choose the type of joint between the upright and the lower crosspiece. Choose joint A or joint B (figure 2).

485. Effects
A. joining of crosspieces against uprights;
B. joining of uprights against crosspieces.

Length of minizinken half-tooth

Length of half the tooth of the minizinken mill.

Counter-profile overlap

Part of the profile that overlaps the counterprofile when the upright and crosspiece are joined.

Lower crossbar counter-profile overlap

COUNTERPROFILE_LOWER_RAIL_OVERLAY

Part A of the profile that overlaps the counterprofile (figure 3) when the upright and lower crosspiece are joined.

Pocketing joint overlap

OVERLAY_POCKETING

Depth of the pocketing machining operation for the joint, made in the receiving element.

Increase in pocketing size

EXTRA_WIDTH_POCKETING

Increase of the above mentioned depth in order to enlarge the machining operation.

Vertical counterprofile joint overlap

OVERLAY_VERTICAL_COUNTERPROFILE

Part of the profile that overlaps the counterprofile when the upright and crosspiece are joined.

Tenon overlap

CLOSEDTENON_OVERLAY

Length of the part of the closed tenon (stub-tenon) that enters in the mortice; for the side of the frame(doors/windows) Window structure to be fixed to the wall or jamb, on which the window is hung. in which the sash/door is applied.

Lower element tenon overlap

CLOSEDTENON_LOWER_RAIL_OVERLAY

Length of the part of the closed tenon (stub-tenon) that enters in the mortice; for the lower side of the frame.

Tenon overlap on central elements

CLOSEDTENON_CENTRAL_OVERLAY

Length of the part of the closed tenon (stub-tenon) that enters in the mortice; for the central elements of the frame.
