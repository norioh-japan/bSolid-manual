Application method

HW_APPMODE

Used to choose the type of machining operation for creating the seats for the article. Choose the machining operation from the drop-down menuList of options or instruments which expand vertically or horizontally when you click on the drop-down buttons..

Vertical milling operation

Generates interpolated vertical milling operationsMachining operation carried out on the piece side facing upwards, with the tool at right angles to the work table. to create the seat.

Horizontal milling operation

Generates interpolated horizontal milling operationsMachining operation carried out on one of the side faces of the piece, with the tool parallel to the work table. to create the seat.

Horizontal boring operation

Generates horizontal borings.

Vertical linear slot

Generates a simple vertical milling operationMachining operation carried out on the piece side facing upwards, with the tool at right angles to the work table. to create the slot.

Horizontal linear slot

Generates a simple horizontal milling operationMachining operation carried out on one of the side faces of the piece, with the tool parallel to the work table. to create the slot.

Depending on the type chosen, the data defined in the "Data for generating the milling operations" paragraph or the "Data for generating the boring operations" paragraph are used.

Data for generating the milling operations
Distance from end of piece

HW_OFFSET_X

Translation in X of the centre point of the article seat relative to the axis along which that cremone bolt or the latch tip slides, to define the article's x coordinate of applicationA synonym of "program" - software with the specific task of managing data, graphics, etc..

The result varies according to the type of machining operation selected in the previous drop-down menuList of commands (to be selected with the mouse) that develop a sub-menu, i.e. a list of options that allow certain functions/operations to be carried out.: milling, boring or slotting.

For boring, the translation is calculated from position X of the bore centre defined in the parameter HW_X_DRILL1.

Y variance

HW_OFFSET_Y

Translation in Y of the centre point of the article seat relative to the axis along which that cremone bolt(1) Sliding cylinder in the sash closure mechanism (e.g. in the cremone bolt). (2) Type of rotating closure used instead of a key to close doors or windows. or the latch tip slides, to define the article's y coordinate of application.

The result varies according to the type of machining operation selected in the previous drop-down menu: milling, boring or slotting.

Additional Z position

HW_OFFSET_Z

Translation in Z of the centre point of the article seat relative to the axis along which that cremone bolt or the latch tip slides, to define the article's z coordinate of application.

The result varies according to the type of machining operation selected in the previous drop-down menu: milling, boring or slotting.

HW_Z

Coordinate z from the face of the element, to determine the level from which to start the machining operation.

Position of bore 1

HW_X_DRILL1

Coordinate x of the centre of the bore with reference to the edge of the element face to determine the position of the first bore.

A negative valuePredefined / pre-set. Standard information useful for the control, if the requested information is not entered. shifts the bore towards the upper or lower edge of the piece, depending on the type of application chosen - high (Applied at the top) or low (Applied at the bottom).
