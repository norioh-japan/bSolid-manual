Operation

TYP

Used to select the type of surface to be generated.

Intersection

Combines the selected surfaces in line with the intersection points, revealing their internal profile(doors/windows) Profile that is the internal part of the window/door..

The resulting shape is the surface of the internal geometry created by the intersection of the two geometries.

Subtraction

Combines the selected surfaces in line with the intersection points, eliminating the external profile(doors/windows) Profile that is the external part of the window/door. of the second geometry selected.

The resulting shape is the surface of the external part of the first geometry and the internal part of the second geometry.
