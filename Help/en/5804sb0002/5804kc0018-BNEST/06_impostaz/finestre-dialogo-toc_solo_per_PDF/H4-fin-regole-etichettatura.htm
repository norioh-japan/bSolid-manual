Creating associations

1. Click on the button to add a new row.
2. Select the drop-down menuList of options or instruments which expand vertically or horizontally when you click on the drop-down buttons. in the Data item column of table B, and choose the project field you want to associate.
3. Select the drop-down menuList of commands (to be selected with the mouse) that develop a sub-menu, i.e. a list of options that allow certain functions/operations to be carried out. in the Labelling field column of table BLEdit, and choose the application software field to associate it with.
