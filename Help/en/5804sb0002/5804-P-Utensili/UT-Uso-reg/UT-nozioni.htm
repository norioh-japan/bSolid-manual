The environment table

The table contains the list of tools, showing some data arranged by row and by column.

Operations that can be performed in the table:

Arranging the rows

Arrange the list by clicking on the required column. The small white triangle next to the column title indicates that an arrangement operation has been carried out.

The small triangle A pointing upwards indicates an increasing arrangement (e.g. from A to Z or from the smallest valuePredefined / pre-set. Standard information useful for the control, if the requested information is not entered. to the biggest one).
The small triangle B pointing downwards indicates a decreasing arrangement (e.g. from Z to A or from the biggest value to the smallest one).

Making the columns bigger or smaller

To make the columns bigger, position the cursor between two titles of the column. When the double arrow appears, press the left mouse key and drag the cursor.

Configuring the table columns

To add or remove columns from the table, proceed as follows:

1. Click on the button to visualise the menuList of commands (to be selected with the mouse) that develop a sub-menu, i.e. a list of options that allow certain functions/operations to be carried out. with the list of columns.

3. Enter the required dimensions (in pixels) or leave the word Auto in the empty text box next to the column name.
4. Click on the button to save the column configuration.
Visualising the card of the tools-aggregates-deflectors

Click twice on a table row to see the card with the data relating to the tool, aggregate or deflector.
