Linear elements

Y movement of the mobile supports that are not references

Distance A (figure 1) between the rod of the clampsPressure exerted by the presser roller to hold the edgebanding strip in place. that don't act as a reference, and the edge of the piece.

This data item is therefore used to lock and machine pieces that are not perfectly linear.

437. Representation of distance A
Minimum distance to lock the pieces in relation to the left side

Minimum distance A (figure 2) that the clamp must maintain from the left side vertex of the piece during the locking phase.

438. Representation of distance A
Minimum distance to lock the pieces in relation to the right side

Minimum distance A (figure 3) that the clamp must maintain from the right side vertex of the piece during the locking phase.

439. Representation of distance A
Maximum piece protrusion from clamp

The maximum protrusion A (figure 4) of the piece from the vertex of the clamp washer, in order to be locked.

440. Representation of distance A
Do not place clamps near hardware machining operations

Enables or disables the positioning of the clamps away from hardware machining operations.

  •    Box enabledClick on a check box to tick it and thereby enable that function.: the clamps are not positioned close to the points where the hardware machining operations are applied, but distributed along the piece.
  •    Box disabledClick on a check box to remove the tick and thereby disable that function.: the clamps are positioned close to the points where the hardware machining operations are applied.
