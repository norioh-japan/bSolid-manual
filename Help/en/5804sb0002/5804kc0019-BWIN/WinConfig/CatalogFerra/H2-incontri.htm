Striker plate data

Description

Depending on the type of buttonCommand field that is responsive to mouse clicks and that enables operations to be activated. selected, this allows to catalogue various types of striker plate(doors/windows) Metal component installed on the frame that allows the locking mechanisms on the sash to close and/or lock the window.:

  •    Strike by pressure
  •    Strike for bolt(1) Sliding cylinder in the sash closure mechanism (e.g. in the cremone bolt). (2) Type of rotating closure used instead of a key to close doors or windows.
  •    Strike for prod
  •    Bolt for prod
  •    Strike for incorrect movement device
  •    Shim piece to lift the tilting window(doors/windows) Sash with double opening (inward-swinging and tilting). See inward-swinging mechanism and tilt-and-turn mechanism.
  •    Strike for mushroom bolt
  •    Strike for tilt

X variance in relation to bolt

HW_OFFSET_X

Translation in X of the centre point of the article seat relative to the axis along which that cremone bolt or the latch tip slides, to define the article's x coordinate of applicationA synonym of "program" - software with the specific task of managing data, graphics, etc..

The result varies according to the type of machining operation selected in the previous drop-down menuList of commands (to be selected with the mouse) that develop a sub-menu, i.e. a list of options that allow certain functions/operations to be carried out.: milling, boring or slotting.

For boring, the translation is calculated from position X of the bore centre defined in the parameter HW_X_DRILL1.

Y variance in relation to bolt

HW_OFFSET_Y

Translation in Y of the centre point of the article seat relative to the axis along which that cremone bolt or the latch tip slides, to define the article's y coordinate of application.

The result varies according to the type of machining operation selected in the previous drop-down menu: milling, boring or slotting.
