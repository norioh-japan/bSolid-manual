Polygon

Description

Creates a regular polygon (equilateral triangle, rhombus, pentagon, hexagon, etc.) or a star-shaped one, inside the construction circle (inscribed) or outside it (circumscribed).

The shape and size of the polygon will vary according to the number of sides and the construction circumference radius.

General parameters

Type of polygon

TYP

Used to select the type of polygon.

Regular

Creates a regular polygon (equilateral triangle, pentagon, hexagon, etc.).

Star

Creates a star-shaped polygon.

Type of construction

POT

Used to choose the type of construction for defining the polygon dimensions.

With external radius

Creates a polygon on the basis of the radius and the dimensions of the centre of the circumscribed construction circle. See figure 1.

With internal radius

Creates a polygon on the basis of the radius of the circumscribed construction circle and the apothem (the height of the triangles inside the polygon). See figure 2.

Number of sides

NS

Number of sides to be generated.

Central point

Centre X

CX

X co-ordinate of the central point of the polygon construction circle.

Centre Y

CY

Y co-ordinate of the central point of the polygon construction circle.

Centre Z

CZ

Z co-ordinate of the central point of the polygon construction circle.

Dimensions

Radius

R1

Radius of the construction circle circumscribing the regular polygon (not star-shaped).

44. Regular polygon

r1.  Radius
a.  Internal radius (apothem)

Internal radius (apothem)

AP

Height of the triangles inside the polygon.

In the construction of the regular polygon (figure 1), it represents the apothem of the polygon itself, hence the height of its internal triangles.

In the construction of the star-shaped polygon (figure 2), it represents the height of the internal triangles (apothem) of the construction polygon inscribing the star-shaped polygon.

Internal radius

R2

Radius of the construction circle circumscribing the star-shaped polygon.
