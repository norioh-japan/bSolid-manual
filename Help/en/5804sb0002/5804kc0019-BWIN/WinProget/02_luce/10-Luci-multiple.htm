Multiple openings

Description

Creates lots of spaces (rough openings) for designing several windows of different shapes.

Associated procedures
1. Click(1) Indicates the action of pressing (and immediately releasing) the mouse button on an object (icon, button, etc.) located under the pointer on the screen. (2) (verb) Pressing and immediately releasing the left mouse button to enable the function of a selected command. on the required shape in the "Opening" data fieldA defined area containing data or commands (data field or command field)..
2. Define the data in the cards below: Parameters; Distances between wall and frame; Positioning.
3. Confirm by clicking on the Ok buttonCommand field that is responsive to mouse clicks and that enables operations to be activated..
4. Click on another shape to generate new openings.
5. Click on the arrow in the "Position" data field to establish where to position the new shape.
6. Define the data in the cards below: Parameters; Distances between wall and frame; Position.
7. Confirm by clicking on the Ok button.

In this way, several windows can be designed simultaneously.
