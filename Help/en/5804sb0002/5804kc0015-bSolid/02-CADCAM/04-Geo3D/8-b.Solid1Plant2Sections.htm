Surface from 2 sections and 1 closed path

Description

Creates a surfaceFigures of a three-dimensional area, that represent a thin open or closed structure. by joining the following geometric elements:

  •    a closed geometric figureFlat figure in which the first and last extremities coincide (they meet). that represents the plan of the surface seen from above.
  •    two free, open outlines that, together with the reference segments, determine the shape of the objectGeneral term used to define any drawing created in the drawing area (e.g. piece, texts, flat figures, etc.)..
  •    two reference segments whose extensionIndicates the type of file (i.e. the type of application) that the file is associated with. Consists of 3 or 4 characters preceded by a dot. For example, the extension "txt" indicates a text-only file that can be read with any text Editor. must intercept the closed geometric figure. The segments are the references for generating the shape of the two planes perpendicular to the plan of the surface. The point where the two segments intercept is the vertex at which the two planes intercept.
