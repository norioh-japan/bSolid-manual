Rounding of edgebanding strip on corners

Description of the machining operation

Used to round off the corners of the edgebanded piece.

Lead-in

Transfer [mm]

Used to transfer the centre B of the circle arc along its bisector, to modify the rounding on the corner. The values of the initial point C and the final point D of the arc will automatically change (figure 4).

Data check

EDC

Enables (box ticked) or disables the machine data check, to check the data tally with the programmed machining operation.

Enables (box ticked) or disables the activation of the blowers to remove woodchips from the edgebanding strips.
