Feed speed

Feed

Advance speed of the tool during the machining operation.

Descent speed

PlungeFeed

Speed at which the tool moves when performing the following operations:

  •    move from the safety position A to the surfaceFigures of a three-dimensional area, that represent a thin open or closed structure. of the piece B, before starting the machining operation;
  •    exit the piece at the end of the machining operation and return the safety position A.
248. Representation of the data
A. Safety position
B. Piece surface
Lead-in/lead-out speed

RetractFeed

This data is used when defining the types of lead-in and lead-out in the data field "Type".

Speed at which the tool moves when performing the following operations:

  •    make the entry tract during the descent;
  •    make the exit tract during the ascent after the machining operation.
Rotation speed

RotationRate

Tool rotation speed (rpm).

Z safety position

ZSic

Z position for establishing the distance that the tool must maintain from the piece during unit movement/release operations, in order to avoid any collisions with the table elements or the piece itself.

Z pushing position

Zacc

Z position for establishing the distance of the tool from the initial machining point. The tool arrives (at maximum speed) at the position established in this data field, then slows down to reach the initial machining point.
