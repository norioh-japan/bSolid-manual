Steps for drawing an ellipse
1. Click(1) Indicates the action of pressing (and immediately releasing) the mouse button on an object (icon, button, etc.) located under the pointer on the screen. (2) (verb) Pressing and immediately releasing the left mouse button to enable the function of a selected command. on the buttonCommand field that is responsive to mouse clicks and that enables operations to be activated. of the drawing barBar listing the drawing tools..
2. Choose the type of ellipse by clicking on one of the two tools in the "Type of ellipse" data fieldA defined area containing data or commands (data field or command field)..

3. Insert the central point of the ellipse by clicking in the drawing areaArea dedicated to the creation and modification of the geometries/machining operations..
To specify the point or dimensions of the ellipse, use the relevant fields of the data area.

4. Click on the point where you want to end the ellipse.
5. Click on the Undo button to close the Ellipse card.

General parameters

Type of ellipse

ELT

Used to select the type of ellipse.

Insertion of two points to define the dimension

Creates an ellipse passing through two points.

Insertion of centre point and length of the two semi-axes

Creates an ellipse using the co-ordinates of the centre and the length of the semi-axes as reference points.

Rotation

R

Rotation of the ellipse on the xy construction planePlane on which a flat geometry is constructed. The default plane is the XY plane.. Enter the required angle.

Initial dimension point

Start X

X1

X co-ordinate of the ellipse start point.

Start Y

Y1

Y co-ordinate of the ellipse start point.

Start Z

Z1

Z co-ordinate of the ellipse start point.

Central point

Centre X

CX

X co-ordinate of the ellipse central point.

Centre Y

CY

Y co-ordinate of the ellipse central point.

Centre Z

CZ

End dimension point

End X

X2

X co-ordinate of the ellipse end point used to establish its dimensions.

End Y

Y2

Y co-ordinate of the ellipse end point used to establish its dimensions.

End Z

Z2

Z co-ordinate of the ellipse end point used to establish its dimensions.

Semi-axis length

Semi-axis 1

R1

Horizontal ellipse semi-axis, on the construction plane. In the case of the xy plane, it is along the x axis.

Semi-axis 2

R2

Vertical ellipse semi-axis, on the construction plane. In the case of the xy plane, it is along the y axis.
