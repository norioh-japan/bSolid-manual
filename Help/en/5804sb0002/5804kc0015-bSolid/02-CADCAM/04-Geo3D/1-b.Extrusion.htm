General parameters

Geometries to extrude

PTE

Used to select the geometries to extrude.

The selected geometry determines the base of the object (length and width) which is extruded upwards.

Height

H

Height of the three-dimensional figureGeometric figure of a three-dimensional space (e.g. the surfaces)..

Angle

A

Angle of inclination of the sides of the three-dimensional figure, calculated based on the line perpendicular to the construction surface.

Rotation

R

Angle of rotation of the upper side of the three-dimensional figure in relation to the construction surface to generate a torsion.

Mode

Opening

OB

Allows the user to choose whether to create a three-dimensional closed or open figure above, below, or on both sides.

Open

Three-dimensional open figure (without upper and lower face). In this case, an open surface is generated.

Closed

Three-dimensional closed figure. In this case, a closed surface is generated.

Open below

Three-dimensional figure without lower face. In this case, an open surface is generated.

Open above

Three-dimensional figure without upper face. In this case, an open surface is generated.

Accuracy

PR

Index to define the precision of the three-dimensional figure created. Values lower than 1,000 make the object more precise.
