Program name

Name of the machining program imported in the specific article management area (see Articles ).

Priority

Numerical value that establishes the priority level of each single article.

The higher the number, the higher the priority. An article of level 40 is considered of greater priority than an article of level 30, and the profiles it contains are positioned first.

Margin around the profile, for positioning it on the sheet so it is left slightly detached from the adjacent profiles.
