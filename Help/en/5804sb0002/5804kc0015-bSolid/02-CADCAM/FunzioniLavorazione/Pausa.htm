Avoid suspension step

SWS

This data field is enabled by selecting the "Without transfer" option in the "Enable transfer" data field and by disabling the "Piece release" check box.

Disables (box ticked) or enables the suspension of the program between a machining and the next. In this case, you can rotate or tilt the piece.
