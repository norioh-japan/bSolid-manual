Hinge data for pivot sash/door

Button for creating article:

Description

Allows to categorize the type of hinges to be applied to pivot opening(doors/windows) Type of opening that rotates the sash around the semi-central horizontal axis (pivot window) or semi-central vertical axis (rotating window) of the window. This type of opening rotates the sash by moving one part outward and a part inward. windows.

Cross correction

HW_CORR_TRASV

Allows to correct the position of the hinge, which is applied to the centre of the upright as standard.

• A positive valuePredefined / pre-set. Standard information useful for the control, if the requested information is not entered. moves the hinge toward the interior of the sash upright.
• A negative value moves the hinge toward the exterior of the sash upright.

Y variance

HW_Y_FRAME

Coordinate x of the centre of the bore with reference to the edge of the element face to determine the position of the bore on the upright of the frame.

If two bores have been created, the reference point is the centre of the two bores.
