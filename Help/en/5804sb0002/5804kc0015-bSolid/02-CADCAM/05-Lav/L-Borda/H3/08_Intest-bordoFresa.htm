Edgebanding strip trimming with milling cutter

Description of the machining operation

Used to cut the edgebandingSet of operations for covering the edge of the piece (gluing, cleaning, rounding off, etc.). strip at the ends of the edgebanded side, using a milling cutter.

Allows to define the angle A of the tool starting from the perpendicular to the bisector B of the edge to be machined in order to slightly rotate the tool.
