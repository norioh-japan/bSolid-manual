Navigation bar

Left-hand side bar containing a list of the buttonsCommand field that is responsive to mouse clicks and that enables operations to be activated. for accessing the various archive management cards.

Sequences

Opens the card, for managing and cataloguing the machining sequences.

Automatic machining operations

Opens the card, for managing and cataloguing the automatic machining operations.

MacroProgram part created to execute certain instructions automatically. Can be called up when needed. Once it has been launched, a macro exploits the program interface to perform a sequence of operations programmed by the user.

Opens the card for managing and cataloguing the macros.
