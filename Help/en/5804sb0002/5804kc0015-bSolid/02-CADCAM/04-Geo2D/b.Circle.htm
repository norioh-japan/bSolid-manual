1. Click(1) Indicates the action of pressing (and immediately releasing) the mouse button on an object (icon, button, etc.) located under the pointer on the screen. 3. Insert the central point of the circle by clicking in the drawing areaArea dedicated to the creation and modification of the geometries/machining operations..

General parameters

Type of circle

TYP

Used to select the type of circle.

Circle using 3 Points

Creates a circle passing through three points.

Circle given centre and radius

Creates a circle using the co-ordinates of its central point and radius as reference values.

Circle given centre and diameter

Creates a circle using the co-ordinates of its central point and diameter as reference values.

Circle given 2 Points and radius

Creates a circle using two points and the radius as reference values.

Centre position

ALG

Position of the centre of the circle in relation to the two construction points.
Select one of the two available options.

Pos. 0

Pos. 1
