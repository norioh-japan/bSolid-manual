Using the mouse in the environment Cad/Cam

1. Press the Ctrl key.

3. To continue the selection process, keep the Ctrl key pressed and click on other elements.

Using the right key

The right key of the mouse can be used for the following operations:

  •    Display the quick menuList of commands (to be selected with the mouse) that develop a sub-menu, i.e. a list of options that allow certain functions/operations to be carried out. by clicking on the drawing area or on an outline being created.

Using the central key

The central key of the mouse can be used for the following operations:

  •    Zoom the display of the objects in or out.

Procedure:

1. Press the central mouse key (scroll wheel).
2. Rotate the scroll wheel to zoomProcess of reducing or enlarging the graphic display. in or out on the objects.
  •    Move the display of the objects.

Procedure:

1. Press the central mouse key (scroll wheel) to display the hand cursor.
2. Keep the central key (scroll wheel) pressed, then move the mouse to shift the display and drag the object in the required direction.
  •    Rotate the view associated with the Ctrl key of the keyboard.

Procedure:

1. Press the Ctrl key of the keyboard and the centre key of the mouse (scroll wheel).
2. Keeping both pressed, move the cursor.
