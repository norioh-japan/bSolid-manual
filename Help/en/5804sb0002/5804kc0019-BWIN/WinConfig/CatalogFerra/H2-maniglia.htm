Handle bore execution method

HW_APPMODE

Used to choose the type of machining operation for creating the handle seat. Choose the type of machining operation in the drop-down menuList of options or instruments which expand vertically or horizontally when you click on the drop-down buttons..

Not present

Seat not present.

Milling

Generates interpolated milling operations to create the seat.

Slot only

Generates the straight milling operations for the slot.

Bore and slot

Generates a milling operation (for the slot) and a boring operation.

Generate facing hammer bores

HW_ABIL_MART

Enables (box ticked) or disables the visualisation of the graphics for generating the three Martellina type handle seats.

Diameter of handle bore

HW_D_PERNO

Diameter of the seat for inserting the central pin of the handle (figure 2).

Depth of handle bore

HW_DP_PERNO

Depth of the seat for inserting the central pin of the handle.

Execute the through bore for the handle

HW_PERNO_THR

Enables (box ticked) or disables through boring.

In this case, the through bore is added to the value indicated in the "Depth of handle bore" data field.

Diameter of facing hammer bores

HW_D_MART

Diameter of the two seats for inserting the two Martellina type handle pins (figure 2).

Depth of facing hammer bores

HW_DP_MART

Depth of the two seats for inserting the two Martellina type handle pins.

Distance between handle bore and facing hammer bore

HW_SH

Centre distance between the seat of the central pin and one of the two seats for the Martellina type handle pins (figure 2).
