General data

Description of the tab

Indicates the dimensional data and the data relating to the machining operations in general.

General

The name of the tool.

Diameter

Working length

UsefulLength

Length of the tool.

For boring tools, the length is the distance between the lower tip of the tool and the nose of the spindle (ref. A, figure 2).

For milling tools, the length is the distance between the lower tip of the tool and the nose of the electrospindle (ref. B, figure 2).

Indicates whether the dimensions of the tool are such to prevent other tools from being housed in a tool holder adjacent to the one housing it.

The options in the drop-down menuList of options or instruments which expand vertically or horizontally when you click on the drop-down buttons. show the default list of codes concerning the overall dimensions of the tool; choose the one required:

0

Management disabled.

1

Small sized tool that only occupies the tool holder in which it is housed, without invading the positions of the other tool holders.

2

Medium sized tool that occupies the position of the tool holder where it is housed and the position of the tool holder that follows it.

3

Medium sized tool that occupies the position of the tool holder where it is housed and the position of the tool holder that precedes it.

4

Large sized tool that occupies, in addition to the position of the tool holder where it is housed, and that of the tool holder that precedes and follows it.

This data is used during the automatic housing of the tool in the magazines defined as "random". The field is not used for boring tools.
