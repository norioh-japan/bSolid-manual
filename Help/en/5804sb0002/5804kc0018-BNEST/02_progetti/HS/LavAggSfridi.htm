Crumbling of rest

The card Technology shows only the data fieldsA defined area containing data or commands (data field or command field). relating to specific milling operations to cut the residual parts to reject.
For the description, see Milling (environmentCad/Cam).

Enable crumbling of rest

Enables (box tickedClick on a check box to tick it and thereby enable that function.) or disables the modification of the following data fields.

The following data fields allow you to cut the part left over from the machining of the panel/sheet, in order to remove it from the work table.

X step

Centre distance along the X-axis, to define the distance between cuts.

Y step

Centre distance along the Y-axis, to define the distance between cuts.

Additional tract

Distance from the edge of the panel/sheet, to establish the starting point of the cut.

Minimum length

Minimum length of the milling operation in order to obtain the residual part to be eliminated. If the length of the milling operation is less than this valuePredefined / pre-set. Standard information useful for the control, if the requested information is not entered., the machining operation is not carried out.

Safety distance

Distance between the edges of the machined profiles and the milling operation to obtain the wastePart left over after a machining operation (residue). In nesting projects this definition also applies to waste..

Execution order

Allows you to define when to perform the machining operation Crumbling of rest", i.e. if at the beginning of the program, or at the end, etc.
