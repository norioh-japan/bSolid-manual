Allows to decentralize two slots created.

Since the dimension of the two slots is calculated starting from the centre of the counterplate and the values entered in the field "Distance between 2 slots "and in the field "Length", by inserting a non-zero value, the central reference point is moved and consequently the slots redimensioned.
