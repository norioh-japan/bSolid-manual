Elements

Excess materials
Outside

EXTRA_THICKNESS_EXTERNAL

Excess material(nesting) Type of material to be associated with the articles and sheets of the nesting project. to be removed from the outer side of the piece.

Inside

EXTRA_THICKNESS_INTERNAL

Excess material to be removed from the inner side of the piece.

Right

EXTRA_THICKNESS_RIGHT

Excess material to be removed from the right-hand side of the piece.

Left

EXTRA_THICKNESS_LEFT

Excess material to be removed from the left side of the piece.

Edge trimming cuts on the heads

CUT_EXTRA_THICKNESS

Used to define which side faces (heads) to cut in order to square up the piece.

Calculation of extra material from tool

EXTRA_THICKNESS_TOOL_CALCULATION

Allows to add excess material on the side of the element to be cut to obtain the glazing bead.

Automatic

The excess material is automatically added based on the type of tool used.

Cutting thickness

The excess material is added according to the thickness of the tool used.

Section
Movement in Z on lower element

LOWER_RAIL_ZOVERLAY

Distance from position Z of the frame(doors/windows) Window structure to be fixed to the wall or jamb, on which the window is hung. in order to move the piece.

457. Distance A of the lower element
Frame thickness

THICKNESS

Thickness A (figure 2) of the pieces that make up the frame.

458. Element thickness and width
A. Thickness of the piece: upright or crosspiece(doors/windows) Also: crosspiece. Horizontal part of the window/door.
B. Width of the piece: upright or crosspiece.
Thickness of lower crossbar

LOWER_RAIL_THICKNESS

Thickness A (figure 2) of the lower rail.

Section

SECTION

Width B (figure 2) of the pieces that make up the frame.

Section of lower crossbar

LOWER_RAIL_SECTION

Width B (figure 2) of the lower frame crosspiece.

Section of upper crossbar

UPPER_RAIL_SECTION

Width B (figure 2) of the upper frame crossbar.

Section of glazing bead

FRAME_GLAZING_BEAD_SECTION

Width of the strip used to hold the glass.

Section of central crossbars

CENTRALRAIL_SECTION

Width B (figure 2) of the central frame crosspieces.

Section of central uprights

FRAME_CENTRAL_STILE_SECTION

Width B (figure 2) of the central frame uprights.

Adjacent
Section of the adjacent elements

STRUCTURE_ADJACENT_SECTION

Width of the vertical and horizontal elements (e.g. skirting(doors/windows) Reinforcing crosspiece of the window frame, positioned above the lower crosspiece.).

Overlap between frame and adjacent elements

STRUCTURE_OVERLAY_ADJACENT

Part of the frame that overlaps the vertical or horizontal element when the two elements are joined.

Overlap between lower frame element and adjacent elements

STRUCTURE_OVERLAY_LOWER_ADJACENT

Part of the lower crosspiece that overlaps the skirting during the joining of the two elements.

Overlap between adjacent elements

ADJACENT_OVERLAY_ADJACENT

Part of a vertical or horizontal piece that overlaps the one next to it when they are joined.
