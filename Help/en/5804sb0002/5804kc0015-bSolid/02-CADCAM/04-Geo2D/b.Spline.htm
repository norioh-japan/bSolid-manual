Steps for drawing a FreeForm
1. Click(1) Indicates the action of pressing (and immediately releasing) the mouse button on an object (icon, button, etc.) located under the pointer on the screen. (2) (verb) Pressing and immediately releasing the left mouse button to enable the function of a selected command. on the buttonCommand field that is responsive to mouse clicks and that enables operations to be activated. of the drawing barBar listing the drawing tools..
2. Choose the type of spline by clicking on one of the two tools in the "FreeForm type" data fieldA defined area containing data or commands (data field or command field)..
3. Insert the start point of the FreeForm by clicking in the drawing areaArea dedicated to the creation and modification of the geometries/machining operations..
To specify the start point, use the relevant fields of the data area.
4. Drag the cursorMobile element that is visible on the screen, and which moves as the mouse is moved. to draw, then click in the drawing area to fix the other points of the FreeForm.

5. Click on the point where you want to end the FreeForm. To close it, click on the button.
6. Click on the Undo button to close the FreeForm card.
